Product Requirements Document (PRD): Online Medical Consultation App
1. Overview
A web-based medical platform to enable online video consultations between doctors and patients, with integrated video calls, patient history tracking, and appointment scheduling.

2. Objectives
Enable secure, real-time video consultations via browser.

Maintain comprehensive medical records for each patient.

Facilitate appointment booking with reminders.

Prioritize ease of use, data security, and scalability.

3. Target Users
Patients seeking remote medical consultations.

Independent doctors and clinics offering telemedicine.

4. Core Features
4.1 Video Consultation
Embedded video call interface using WebRTC (or Daily/LiveKit).

Camera, microphone, and screen sharing support.

Note-taking sidebar for doctors during consultation.

Chat component for text messages.

Optional consultation timer.

4.2 Patient Medical History
Timeline view of past consultations.

Secure access to doctor’s notes, prescriptions, and reports.

Optional document upload (PDFs, images).

4.3 Appointment Scheduling
Calendar-based interface to pick available slots.

Email and/or WhatsApp reminders (optional).

Manual or auto-confirmation by doctors.

5. UI/UX Components (ShadCN)
Card components for patient info.

Calendar for scheduling.

Dialog for joining or ending calls.

Tabs to switch between notes/history.

Textarea with autosave for doctor notes.

Badge for consultation status.

Avatar for doctor/patient profile photo.

6. Tech Stack Suggestions
Frontend: Next.js (App Router), Tailwind CSS, shadcn/ui

Video Engine: WebRTC / Daily / LiveKit

Auth: Clerk or NextAuth.js

Data: Prisma ORM (PostgreSQL or SQLite)

Notifications: Email (SMTP) + WhatsApp API

7. Security
Role-based access: Patient / Doctor / Admin

End-to-end encryption for video and data

GDPR or local equivalent compliance

8. Success Metrics
Number of monthly consultations

Average wait time from booking to consultation

Patient satisfaction and retention rate
